About Arcanium

By eliminating intermediaries, reducing transaction costs, and providing a transparent and secure platform, Arcanium plans to make real estate transactions more accessible and efficient. Our solution addresses key issues in both high-income countries (HICs) and low-income countries (LICs), including the challenges of proving property ownership, accessing mortgages, and navigating confusing legal frameworks.

Process of Tokenization

Property Valuation

A certified appraiser evaluates the property and determines its market value.

NFT Creation

The property is tokenized onto the blockchain as a NFT.

Marketplace Listing

The tokenized property is listed on the platform for users to buy and sell.

Smart Contracts

A smart contract is used to manage the distribution and transfer of funds during sale.
